What is this?

A multidimensional explorer of WVS survey data from WVS.

The parallel coordinates display percentage of country population that expressed confidence in different organizations. In other words; the percentage of respondees that expressed "great deal of confidence" or "quite a lot of confidence" which were the two highest tiers on a likert scale with four options. The lines corresponding to each country is colored by continent belonging (according to the United Nations definitions).

Brush the visualization to update other charts on this page.

Let me know what you think on Reddit.

Controls

Brush: Drag vertically along an axis.
Remove Brush: Tap the axis background.
Reorder Axes: Drag a label horizontally.
Invert Axis: Tap an axis label.
Remove Axis: Drag axis label to the left edge.
