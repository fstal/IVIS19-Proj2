What is this?

The parallel coordinates display percentage of country population that expressed confidence in different organizations on the y-axes. In other words; the percentage of respondees that expressed "great deal of confidence" or "quite a lot of confidence" which were the two highest tiers on a likert scale with four options. The lines corresponding to each country is colored by continent-belonging (according to the United Nations definitions).

Brush the visualization to update other charts on this page.

When you have discovered some countries of interest for a specific wave, you can select those through clicking them in the sample list on your right. Select a dataset from the dropdown menu and hit compare to get corresponding gapminder data.
Gapminder data uses the first year of the selected wave as a comparator.

Controls

Brush: Drag vertically along an axis.
Remove Brush: Tap the axis background.
Reorder Axes: Drag a label horizontally.
Invert Axis: Tap an axis label.
Remove Axis: Drag axis label to the left edge.
